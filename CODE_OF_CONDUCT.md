# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in BrowserBridge a respectful and welcoming experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

Participants are encouraged to:

- Be respectful
- Be constructive
- Assume good intent
- Focus on technical discussions
- Help newcomers

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples include:

- Harassment
- Personal attacks
- Discrimination
- Threats
- Deliberate disruption

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct.

They may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or participation that violates these standards.

## Reporting

Report concerns privately to:

conduct@browserbridge.dev
